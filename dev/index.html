Web dev

As a web developer I like to work together with people and initaitves that are into creative uses of web technology and that like unconventional designs. But I'm also open for more traditional stuff. The important thing for me is to help folks manage their data and content themselves instead of relying on commercial third-party providers or cloud solutions. Being independent of platforms and hosting applications yourself is often not exactly easy or low-threshold for laypeople, which is why I am happy to help facilitate digital sovereignty.

Here's a couple of websites that I built or helped building recently:
  • Website for the left-wing library and think tank translib {using Next.js + Tailwind + Headless Wordpress} [design by Lena Haubner]
  • Portfolio page for Markus Streb {done with Ghost}
  • Page for my DIY event crew Income Tax Refund {done with Hugo and Netlify CMS}
  • Implementing new frontend for Entwicklungspolitisches Netzwerk Sachsen e.V. ENS, designed by e.emitu {Custom Wordpress theme}
  • Building a frontend of local DIY online TV Station Hitness Club and implementing a backend (with user-friendly CMS) together with the team. {SvelteKit + Tailwind + Directus CMS + PeerTube}
