Income Tax Refund

For a few years now I'm running a small, non-commercial collective for DIY concerts in Leipzig and around. We set up shows for bands and artists we like and try to create a network of music-lovers, musicians, designers and off-spaces. If you're interested or want to join our newsletter, you can get in touch with us through social media or write to me directly.

Here's our gig archive with nearly all shows we've set up up until now. For obvious reasons there was not a lot happening in 2020 and 2021. But we're continuing.
