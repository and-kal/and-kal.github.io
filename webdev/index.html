Web dev

As a web developer I like to collaborate with people that are into creative uses web technology and that like unconventional designs.

As for frameworks, I usually stick to SvelteKit and Next.js (or React respectively), though I'm always curious to discover new stuff. Other than that, I also like using JAMStack and Static Site Generators for certain projects. And sometimes plain old PHP development also has its place in my toolbox.

Here's a couple of websites that I built or helped building recently:
  • rolfblumig.rocks design by lmnh - büro für gestaltung
  • violetgreensmusic.com design by lmnh - büro für gestaltung
  • pracht-ev.netwith Jonas Holfeld and Judith Crasser
...and here's some stuff I'm currently working on:
  • Refurbishing the frontend of local DIY online TV Station Hitness Club and implementing a user-friendly CMS together with the team.
  • Rebuilding the Sphere Radio, enhancing the browsing experience via single page app strategies and server-side rendering.
  • Building a portfolio page for Holzwerkstatt Lindenau with layouts by lmnh - büro für gestaltung and illustrations by Studio Goof; also setting up a simple headless CMS
  • Swapping the frontend of critical, left-wing library and think tank translib's blog and going headless.
