Staxl

is my DJ moniker

For a couple of years now I'm in active in a great and applaudable mini-club in Leipzig called PRACHT, which is basically where I've been picking up DJing (more or less) - simply by checking out how the others do it, and even more so by constantly getting introduced to new and exciting styles of music. Some DJs that I've seen playing there and found super inspiring were Cera Khin, Ossia, Judith Crasser NVST, Saulk Regurk and a whole lot more. PRACHT really is a great place for discovering and building networks, so definitely check their newsletter or their Facebook to be kept updated.

Since then I've been doing a couple of mixes for different online Radio stations and such, like: New New World Radio, Radio Super Zoom, Mutant Radio, n10.as, or lahmacun radio.

Other than that I've been playing some sets at events in PRACHT of course, at Seanaps Festival, at mjut, but mostly in underground and DIY spaces that I can't link here, hehe.
